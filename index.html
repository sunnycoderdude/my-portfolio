About Me

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Satinder "Sunny" Singh and I am an aspiring programmer and accomplished financial analyst. My career has spanned a wide variety of roles within global finance.

Following my undergraduate studies at NYU, I spent my analyst years at Deutsche Bank and TPG focused on distressed real estate debt. I made the switch over to the public markets as an associate within the long/short equities group at Citadel. I am currently on an extended garden leave and will be joining Millennium as an analyst covering real estate securities.

I have always had a passion for programming having taken a variety of classes at NYU. While I focused on building my investing career, I kept my programming skills sharp by writing scripts in VBA. I believe the marriage between data science and finance will only continue to strengthen.
